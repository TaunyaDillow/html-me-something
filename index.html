A Truly Global MBA

It began with a simple question...

"Now what?"

I was halfway through my final year of undergrad studies, finally completing my Art History Degree. I spent so much time exploring various courses of study, that I didn't know what I wanted to do once I graduated. There isn't much in the field of Art History that one can do with only a Bachlors Degree and by this time I was sure that I didn't want to continue this path of education. So, with about 3 classes remaining until graduation, I needed a plan.

In a fit of practically, I decided to pursue an MBA. At least that way, I probably could find a job. I found the Global MBA offered by Webster University and decided to apply. Fortunately, I had quite a bit of the required courses already completed so I only needed to take the GRE and purchase a one way ticket to Switzerland!
